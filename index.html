About me

I am a first year PhD in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Texas at Austin. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Sujay Sanghavi. My research interests include theoritical machine learning, optimization, and sequential decision making under uncertainty.

Before moving to Austin, I received the B.E. degree in the Department of Electrical Engineering from Tsinghua University in 2018. You can refer to my cv for more detailed education and research experience.
