About me

I am a second year PhD in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Texas at Austin. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Sujay Sanghavi and Prof. Inderjit Dhillon. My research interests include theoritical machine learning, optimization, and sequential decision making under uncertainty.

Before moving to Austin, I received the B.E. degree in the Department of Electrical Engineering from Tsinghua University in 2018. I also spent a great summer in the University of Alberta in 2017, working on reinforcement learning with Prof. Richard Sutton. In the Spring 2017, I was an exchange student at University of Texas at Austin, and working as an intern in Prof. Peter Stone's research group. You can refer to my CV for more detailed education and research experience.
